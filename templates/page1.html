About VidiFy

VidiFy leverages machine learning (ML) and natural language processing (NLP) to automatically create a short-easily consumable high quality explainer video summarizing any article in seconds. Background images and speech (or music) are automatically selected for you. 

Summarize articles into easily consumable videos

Video has much higher engagement than text articles and is increasingly being used by consumers to make purchasing decisions.
